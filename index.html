The open-source way to invoice in Greece.

Elefthero is a practical, transparent invoicing workspace for Greek sole traders and small businesses—built around AADE myDATA, not lock-in.

View the source

Invoicing should be understandable, usable, and yours.

Many small Greek businesses face expensive, opaque invoicing platforms. Elefthero turns the myDATA process into a clear workflow with a local SQLite data store, readable records, and real AADE test/production modes.

01 — CREATE

Invoices that fit real work

Greek invoice types, multiple line items, mixed VAT, exemption reasons, payment methods, series and sequential numbering.

02 — VERIFY

Know your customer

Reusable clients with VAT data, VIES validation and ΓΕΜΗ enrichment when those public services are available.

03 — TRANSMIT

See exactly what happened

Generate AADE myDATA XML, submit it to the chosen AADE environment, then preserve the returned XML, MARK, UID and QR URL.

A transparent path from draft to AADE.

1. ConfigureSet issuer details, credentials, mode and invoice defaults in a guided setup.
2. Add a clientStore VAT, address and optional public business information for reuse.
3. Build the invoiceEnter lines, VAT, classifications and the payment method.
4. Review XMLInspect generated payloads and diagnostic records before and after submission.
5. Keep the proofIssue a browser PDF with its AADE identifiers and clickable QR link.

Built as a working product, with Codex in the loop.

Elefthero is a Flask application designed for daily use, not a mockup. Codex helped shape the implementation across schema-sensitive myDATA XML, a multilingual product surface, deployment and documentation—while the project remained inspectable in a public repository.

Designed for responsible access

AADE credentials are not committed to Git. They are entered during first setup and stored locally by the deployed application. The interface distinguishes AADE test from production submission.
