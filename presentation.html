From draft to proof

A transparent path from invoice to AADE.

1. CONFIGUREBusiness profile, issuer VAT, series, environment, encrypted credentials.
2. BUILDLines, quantity, VAT, exemption reasons, payment method, and E3 classifications.
3. GENERATECreate schema-aware myDATA XML and retain the exact sent payload.
4. TRANSMITSend to real AADE Test or Production and retain the returned XML.
5. KEEPStore MARK, UID, QR URL, PDF, reports, and reusable templates.

06 / 08
Trust and safety

Security should be practical, not performative.

  • AADE, Resend, Turnstile, and TOTP secrets are encrypted at rest and never committed to Git.
  • Passwords are hashed; optional authenticator-app 2FA adds a QR-based TOTP challenge.
  • Every POST action requires a server-validated CSRF token.
  • Password, Turnstile, and OTP failures share configurable login rate limiting.
  • Developer logs retain the technical evidence needed to investigate outcomes.
Accessible by design

The in-app WCAG 2.1-oriented panel lets users adjust text size, contrast, spacing, link emphasis, and motion preference.

It is not a claim of full WCAG conformance—it is a concrete effort to reduce everyday friction.
